## Post Title: Launched a $39 lead list service for local businesses — first paying customer in hour 1.

### What is it?

Dead simple: tell me your niche (e.g., "plumber") and city (e.g., "Cape Town"), and I deliver a targeted CSV of leads within the hour.

- Source: Google Maps (updated, real listings).
- Output: Name, Phone, Address, Email (where available), Website, Rating, Review Count.
- Price: $39 flat for 50-100 leads. $99/mo for agencies (unlimited).
- Turnaround: under 1 hour.

### The Story

My dad's a plumber. He spent $400/month on a lead gen agency. The leads were cold, shared with 2 competitors, and 60% were wrong numbers.

I built a simple scraper in Python to pull local business listings from Google Maps. Within 30 minutes I had 200+ verified contacts in our area. He used it for cold outreach and landed 6 new clients in the first month.

I cleaned it up and packaged it as a service. First paying customer bought a list for dentists in Joburg within an hour of launch.

### Who is it for?

- Service businesses that need fresh, local B2B leads (plumbers, electricians, HVAC, dentists, beauty, gyms, etc.)
- Small agencies that build lead lists manually and want to save time
- SaaS founders with a specific target market (e.g., "dentists in Cape Town")

### Why not just use Apollo/Lusha?

- They're $100+/month
- Their data is global, not local. You get leads from all over.
- Their targeting is broad — you can't lock to one city
- They require subscriptions — we charge per list

For $39, you get exactly what you asked for, delivered this afternoon. No ongoing fees.
