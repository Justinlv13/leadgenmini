About LeadGenMini

LeadGenMini started as a simple tool to help South African businesses find more customers without paying R500+/month for overpriced lead databases filled with stale, shared contacts.

Instead of buying old data from agencies, we built a scraper that pulls fresh business contact information directly from Google Maps. Every lead includes the business name, phone number, email (where available), website, address, and Google rating.

Today we serve customers in South Africa, the UK, the US, and Australia. We have over 38,000 leads in our database covering every major city and business niche.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fresh is the data?

All leads are scraped from Google Maps within 24 hours of your order delivery. If we don't already have leads for your specific niche and city combination in our database, we scrape them specifically for you and deliver within 24 hours.

What format do I receive?

Starter and Pro packages include a CSV file. The Agency package also includes VCF format for importing into your phone or CRM. All files are ready to upload directly into your email or sales tool.

Is the data accurate?

Leads are verified at the time of scraping. Business names, addresses, and phone numbers come directly from Google Maps listings. Email addresses are included where publicly listed on the business website. Bounce rates are typically under 10%.

Can I use this data for cold email?

Yes. You own the data after purchase and can use it for outbound sales, email marketing, or any legitimate business purpose. We recommend complying with local data protection regulations (POPIA in South Africa, GDPR in the UK, CAN-SPAM in the US).

Do you offer refunds?

If we cannot deliver your leads within the promised timeframe, you receive a full refund. If more than 20% of the emails in your list hard bounce, we will re-scrape and resend your list at no charge.

How does country-specific pricing work?

We adjust pricing based on purchasing power in your country. South African customers pay approximately 40% of the US price. Australian customers pay 70%. UK customers pay 85%. This makes the data accessible to businesses in every market we serve.

What niches are available?

We cover all business types that list on Google Maps: marketing agencies, recruitment firms, insurance brokers, plumbers, dentists, HVAC technicians, electricians, law firms, accountants, restaurants, and more. If it shows up on Google Maps, we can scrape it.

What if I need leads in a city you don't cover?

Let us know. We can scrape any city in South Africa, the UK, the US, or Australia within 24 hours. Just specify your target city and niche when ordering.
